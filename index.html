About the club

Founded in 1983 in the heart of Meru, Al Azizia Football Club has been a beacon of football excellence for over four decades. With a rich history of nurturing talent and fostering a strong sense of community, we have become a beloved institution in the world of football. Our commitment to developing players both on and off the field has earned us a reputation for excellence and sportsmanship. Join us as we continue to build on our legacy and strive for greatness in every match we play.
